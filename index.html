About Us

At Chalk & Board, we believe that every student has the potential to achieve greatness. Our mission is to provide a nurturing and inspiring learning environment where students can unlock their full potential, build confidence, and excel academically.

Founded with a passion for education, we offer personalized coaching in Math, Science and English for classes 5 and 6. Our experienced and dedicated teachers are committed to helping students not only master their subjects but also develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and lifelong learning skills.

Why Choose Us?

  • Personalized Attention: Small class sizes ensure that every student receives individualized support.
  • Expert Faculty: Our teachers are highly qualified and passionate about teaching.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: We offer well-structured courses designed to meet the needs of students at all levels.
  • Modern Facilities: Our classrooms are equipped with the latest tools and resources to enhance learning.
  • Proven Results: Our students consistently achieve top grades and gain admission to prestigious institutions.

At Chalk & Board, we don’t just teach—we inspire, motivate, and empower. Join us and take the first step toward a brighter future.
